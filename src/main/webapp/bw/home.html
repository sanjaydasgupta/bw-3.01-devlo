Welcome to the BuildWhiz New Multifamily Project document center

Your documents can be viewed by clicking Documents in the left margin. The documents page allows you to find and download the help you need, and to upload new versions of documents you create if applicable.

RFIs may be initiated and RFI responses viewed by clicking RFI in the left margin. The two numbers in the blurb are (a) count of unread messages, and (b) total messages respectively.

The Status Update page enables you to report progress and file submittals.

To change your password or other personal information click on Profile in the left margin.

More information can be obtained by clicking Help in the left margin. Page-specific help can be obtained by clicking on the button near the top-right corner of any page. You can return to this home page by clicking on your name at the top-left corner.

You can log-out by clicking Logout in the left margin or at the top-right corner.
